### Types of transition
#### 1. Addition (penambahan)
Kata kunci: Furthermore, moreover, in addition, also, another….

Indonesia has suffered with its broken government. **Furthermore,** the corruption spread widely and massively from the top till the bottom of the governance..

### 2. Contrast (perbandingan)
Keyword: However, on the other hand, nevertheless, while, yet, in contrast 

Most lecturers help students with lots of tasks and assignments.. **However,** some of them allow the students to help themselves with self learning environment..

## 3. Cause and effect 
Kata kunci: therefore, as a result, consequently, because of this, thus

Internet connects people and information in sub second. **As a result,** market happens very rapidly with no physical touch point.


## 4. Example (contoh)
Keywords: for example, for instance, such as, namely

Many animals migrate to another part of the world regularly. **For instance,** whales travel thousands of miles to warmer water.

## 5. Summary (kesimpulan)
Keywords: in conclusion, to summarize, overall, in short 

Recycling reduces waste, conserve resources, and saves energy. **In conclusion,** it is an effective way to protect the environment.

Example:
One of the most effective ways to conserve energy is by using renewable resources. **For example,** Solar power, wind energy, and hydropower have become more common worldwide. **As a result,** These alternatives reduce dependence on fossil fuels and help combat climate change.

# Restatement question  =  Paraphrase 
Understanding idea and restate it in new form.
Questions: 
- Which of the following best expresses the best the meaning of the sentence?
- what is the best Restatement of the sentence? 
- which sentence is closest in meaning to the following?

Example: 
1. Using passive sentence
The company produces 1000 units per year

One thousand units were produced by the company last year

2. Using synonyms
We have lecture after Fajr prayer 

A class is held after Fajr prayer
